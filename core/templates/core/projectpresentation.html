General Instructions

  • Event is planned in distinctive manner for both hardware and software with seprate time slots
  • Two different Prize categories for hardware and software, with a total prize pool worth ₹15,000
  • Cash Prizes: 1st - ₹3000, 2nd - ₹2500, 3rd - ₹2000 for each category
  • Projects must be presented using a ppt template of your choice
  • Each team will be allotted 10 minutes for their presentation.
  • The presentation must consist of exactly 7 slides, including the introduction and final slide.
  • Teams must use visual aids such as slides, charts, or videos to support their presentation.
  • Hardware models or prototypes should be demonstrated during the presentation
  • Software projects should include a live demonstration or a detailed walkthrough of the code and functionality.
  • Software projects : 9.00 am to 1.00 pm
  • Hardware projects: 2.00 pm to 4.30 pm

Step into the spotlight with our exciting Project Presentation event!

This is your chance to bring your creative ideas to life. The event is divided into two different categories Hardware and Software projects, with attractive cash prizes for each. Whether you’re building a functional prototype or developing innovative code, now’s your opportunity to shine! Each team will present their projects in a 7-slide PPT format, showcasing their creativity and implementation skills. Top teams will be evaluated on their presentation, and stand a chance to win accolades for their groundbreaking solutions! Ready to make an impact? Register your team of three now and take the stage with your visionary project!
