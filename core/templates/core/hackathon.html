Unleash Your Innovation: 7-Hour Hackathon Challenge!

Welcome to our thrilling 2-round Hackathon, where innovation meets endurance! The event kicks off with a preliminary round and culminates in a 7-hour endurance Hackathon. Check out the released problem statements, and start preparing your solution with the help of the ppt template. In the preliminary round, teams will present their chosen problem statement solutions through a detailed PPT utilizing the providing ppt template. The top teams will then be shortlisted to advance to the main event—an intense 7-hour hackathon where they will develop their solutions live. Winners will be awarded exciting cash prizes for their outstanding efforts!

General Instruction

  • Participants shall choose one of the released problem statements and start preparing
  • Participants can form teams consisting of 4 members, Each team must be registered throught the portal before registration closes.
  • Preliminary round: Teams must submit and present their solutions via the provided template only. Any layout modification to template is strictly prohibited.
  • Preliminary round: Presentations will be evaluated based on creativity, technical feasibility, and the overall approach.
  • The top 30 teams will be shortlisted for the 7 - hour endurance hackathon (finals).
  • During the endurance round, participants must utilize only college lab systems; personal laptops are prohibited.
  • Teams will be evaluated based on the originality of their work, as well as feasibility and scalability factors.
